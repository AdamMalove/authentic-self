🚀 QUICK START (3 STEPS)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: READ README.md
  → Understand philosophy
  → Learn the 15 dimensions
  → See how it works

STEP 2: ASK ALL 52 QUESTIONS (QUESTIONS.md)
  → One at a time
  → Listen for truth, not polish
  → Probe when answers feel rehearsed
  → Follow the checkpoints (Midpoint, Deep Reflection)

STEP 3: SYNTHESIZE IDENTITY.md (SYNTHESIS.md guide)
  → Extract from each of 15 dimensions
  → Look for patterns and contradictions
  → Write in their voice
  → 15 sections total (one per dimension)
