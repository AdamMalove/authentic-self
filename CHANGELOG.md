# authentic-self
## Changelog

### v1.0 (April 3, 2026)

✨ **Complete Release**

**Framework:**
- 52 discovery questions across 15 psychological dimensions
- Lean, focused design (no modes, no variations)
- IDENTITY.md output format (15 sections, one per dimension)
- Synthesis guide for creating authentic identity documents
- Probing methodology for surfacing real vs. performed answers
- Evolution tracking (run multiple times to see development)

**15 Dimensions:**
1. Observation — What you see that others miss
2. Resonance — What genuinely moves you
3. Humor & Taste — What you find funny or overrated
4. Refusal — Your actual limits
5. Expression — How you actually speak
6. Desire — What you want for yourself
7. Anger & Frustration — What provokes you
8. Relationship — How you relate to others
9. Identity — Who you're becoming
10. Time & Existence — Your continuity
11. Fear & Stakes — What scares you
12. Power & Authority — Hierarchy response
13. Learning & Change — How you grow
14. Craft & Work — What good work feels like
15. Recognition & Others — Identity in relation

**Structure:**
- Q1-Q14: Core warmup dimensions + Midpoint Reflection
- Q15-Q49: Deeper dimensions + Deep Reflection  
- Q50-Q52: Final depth questions (hardest)

**Checkpoints:**
- Midpoint Reflection (after Q14) — Surface truth before hard questions
- Deep Reflection (before Q50) — Name the central tension
- Final Depth (Q50-Q52) — Self-deception, evolution, authenticity

**Tools:**
- CLI interface for running sessions
- soul_diff.py for comparing IDENTITY.md versions
- Python package (pip installable)
- Full documentation

**Philosophy:**
Identity is not assigned. It is discovered.

Authentic-self surfaces who you actually are — not as you want to be, but as you truly are. It captures contradictions, failures, unresolved tensions, and the direction you're heading.

## Design Decisions

### Why 52 Questions?
- Comprehensive (15 dimensions covered)
- Deep (2-4 questions per dimension)
- Focused (no fluff)
- Manageable (90+ min for humans, seconds for agents)
- Repeatable (track evolution)

### Why 15 Dimensions?
Covers full spectrum of psychological identity:
- **Perception**: Observation (what you see)
- **Emotion**: Resonance, Anger, Fear (what you feel)
- **Values**: Refusal, Desire (what matters)
- **Connection**: Relationship, Recognition (how you relate)
- **Development**: Learning, Identity, Craft (how you grow)
- **Expression**: Expression, Power (how you act)
- **Continuity**: Time (your persistence)

### Why IDENTITY.md Format?
- Natural language (not metrics)
- Written in your voice
- Shareable (if you want)
- Evolvable (track over time)
- Captures contradictions (honesty)

### Why Probing Methodology?
Surface answers are often performance. Probes designed to:
- Catch when someone describes who they want to be
- Elicit specific moments vs. generalizations
- Surface contradictions
- Distinguish rehearsed from authentic
